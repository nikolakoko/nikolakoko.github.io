My education

Throughout my academic journey, I have consistently excelled. From my days at Kuzman Josifovski-Pitu Elementary School to Rade Jovcevski-Korcagin High School, I maintained a straight-A record. Biology, Mathematics, and Computer Science captivated me the most. I also pursued additional courses in media design at Semos, earning five valuable degrees. Additionally, I had the privilege of participating in a special course called Brain-O-Brain, where I honed my mathematical abilities using an abacus. This led me to participate in three tournaments, where I proudly achieved first place twice and second place once. Now I am a first year student at FINKI Univeristy, UKIM, Skopje.
